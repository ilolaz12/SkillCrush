Hi, my name is Hannah and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to experiment!

Personal projects are your friend. Be curious!

Courses and books can teach you all kinds of wonderful, practical skills, but only by going out and making use of that knowledge will gain you a native user's intuition.

Set up your coding desk.

I cannot reccomend enough, having more than one monitor (or at least a very large screen). There are so many windows to keep track of while coding, not having to sort through multiple tabs will save you a headache.

Wherever you work, make sure it's comfortable and endurable. It's easy to neglect your body, but poor posture will harm you in the long run.
