Advice for New Coders

Google is your bestest of all Best Friends!

Always check Slack first for an answer to your questions. If you are having the problem, someone else has had it too

Make no mistake, the "why" IS important, but it's best to learn it AFTER you master the "how".

You'll just confuse yourself if you learn the "why" before you learn the "how".
