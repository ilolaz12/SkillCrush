How to Keep Moving When Coding!

Mental fatigue is something you may come across more often than you'd like! I've found that the best way to keep one's mind fresh and productive is to take breaks whenever slowing down or hitting a wall. It's also very helpful for your break to not overlap with what you're coding and to not be mentally taxing.

So while you personally might be well served by doing a Sodoku or some reading about an unrelated topic, that could take the form of a brief walk outside, watching some funny videos, or doing your grocery shopping. Or just staring at nothing in particular. Those breaks could be for 5 minutes or for a few hours, depending on how you're feeling at that moment or on your responsibilities for the day. Either way you'll want to take a break, and it's also important to remember to not feel guilty about it as can be one's wont.

Don't burn your candle at both ends!
