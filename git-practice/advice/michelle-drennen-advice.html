Hi, my name is Michelle and I have some advice for you!

Give yourself grace!

You are learning a new language and new skills so be patient with yourself! Give yourself grace and time to figure it out. You got this!

Don't rush through!

It's tempting to want to speed through videos and lessons so you can click the "next" button and finish the lesson but take your time to
really understand. You may not understand everything but really try to understand the big concepts before moving on.

Have fun!

You are doing something new so try to enjoy it and have fun! The Skillcrush community has your back so use your resources and make
the most of the opportunity.
