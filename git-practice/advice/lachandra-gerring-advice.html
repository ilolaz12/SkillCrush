Hi, my name is Skillcrush and I have some advice for you!

Practice, practice, practice!

When learning how to code, it is important to practice coding as much as possible. Find small projects to do. Try to recreate websites, designs etc., that you admire. Watch videos and follow along. The more you code the better and faster you become.

Utilize all available resources

There are blogs, websites, videos, and articles galore on the internet to help you learn. Don't be afraid of Google, it is your best friend. Utilizing all your resources will help you stay current on trends and new techniques.
