Hi, my name is Jacquelyn and I have some advice for you!

Don't give up!

We are our own harshest critics when we are learning something new. It's okay not to understand everything. It's okay to start over. It's okay to do things at a different pace than the people around you. Just keep moving and don't give up! You will make it.

Immerse yourself.

Learning to code is essentially like learning to speak a new language. It may involve learning to speak several new languages! That's not an easy thing to do and you are a rockstar for accepting the challenge.

Thinking about the process as if you were learning a second or third language can give you an edge. If you were learning a new spoken language, you would read it, write it, speak it, have conversations with people who are fluent in the language, etc. Coding is no different! Read code, write code, watch tutorials, talk to people who know the language you want to learn, and ask questions! Immerse yourself in the language. You are learning more than you realize.
