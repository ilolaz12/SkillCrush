Never give up!

You are doing something you've never done before, and therefore, mistakes are inevitable. Dont' give up though! Work through the tough parts and learn from the mistakes (or error messages). :)

In this field, learning will continuous. New technologies are constantly being developed. In order keep up, you are going to need to contiue to hone your skills and practice frequently. Get comfortable with research, reading, and practicing.
