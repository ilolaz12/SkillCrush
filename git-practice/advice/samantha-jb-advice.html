Hi, my name is Samantha and I have some advice for you!

If you're not creative, that's okay!

Neither am I, obviously.

Figure out what you're good at.

And practice!

Practice the things you're bad at too, but spend the time developing the skills you are good at and want to do for work eventually.

My favorite (paraphrased) quote is from the book The ONE Thing by Gary Keller:
"Are you practicing to be your best?
Or are you practicing to be the best?"
