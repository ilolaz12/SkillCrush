Hi, my name is Joe and I have some advice for you!

You're going to make mistakes...

It's O.K. to not be O.K. ... O.K.? Seriously, if this is your first time getting deeper into coding, mistakes keep coming and they don't stop coming. We need to make mistakes in order to learn from them.

Don't be afraid to ask for help...

Especially for those who need that extra boost to go outside themselves. People all around you and on the internet are here to support and guide you on your journey. Who knows - one day YOU will be that helpful friend for that coding newbee just trying to get by!
