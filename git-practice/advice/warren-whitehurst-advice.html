Hi, my name is Warren Whitehurst and I have some advice for you!

Build your success squad

"Success is empty if you arrive at the finish line alone. The best reward is to get there surrounded by winners."
Howard Schultz, Starbucks.

It is absolutely okay to experience feelings of doubt and uncertainty. What gives me drive and hope is the love and support of my family, friends, and mentors that keep me going. I know if I'm having a tough day that their wisdom and understanding is enough to keep me from giving up. Each time, I've managed to get back up and continue and the progress since day one has been incredible. Include those people who will help you stay on the path and root you on to the finish line.

Have fun with the unknown

"If you're prepared and you know what it takes, it's not a risk. You just have to figure out how to get there. There is always a way to get there."
Mark Cuban, Entrepeneur

Going from one industry to another with no prior experience in tech has been a major mental obstable. It's the unknown and lack of confidence at times that has slowed down my personal progress. It's okay for everything to feel strange at first and trust the process. Even if it takes time to gain confidence and the experience, it can and will be life-changing for many and for those reasons, I'm in! Sorry, I had to include one shark tank reference.
