Hi, my name is Heather and I have some advice for you!

Working through frustration helps you become a better coder!

When you are learning to code there are many times when you just dont understand why your code is just not working. It is so easy to get frustrated and discouraged. If you keep trying and researching solutions when you finally figure it out you will have even more understanding of programming than you did before.

Those instances when you are researching and trying things to get it to work will stick with you. Next time you wont make that mistake again or if you see it happen you will know exactly what to do to fix it. Vioala you are becoming a more advanced coder with every problem solved.

Create learning goals to get to your ultimate goal

Knowing where you want to be and breaking it down into steps makes it easier to achieve. Instead of being overwhelmed by the big picture and everything you need to learn to reach your goal. You will have little peices of multiple successes along the way to help you stay motivated.

Having a plan will help to keep you focused on your goal. I know there are so many things I want to learn it is easy to move on to the next thing before you fully understand what you were originally learning. So writing a clear plan will help you get to your goal faster and more efficiently.

Don't forget to have fun!! Learning is supposed to be fun

I know from experience it is easy to just be focused on the end goal and loose sight of enjoying the journey.

If your plans gets set back or needs to be adjusted it is ok. Dont get frustrated become you will get there if you stay focused and positive and have fun learning
