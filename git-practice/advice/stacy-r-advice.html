Hi, my name is Stacy and I have some advice for you!

Keep a Schedule

Schedule your learning time in a calendar because you want to have consistency and continued progress towards this new career path. Life will get in the way and steal your time so be stingy and maintain a schedule and keep your momentum going.

Watch Your Self Talk

Don't be hard on yourself. Just know that you will be confused at times if not most of the time. It comes with the territory. Be nice and patient with yourself. You are learning something completely foreign. Anything worth doing takes time. You are worth it!

Support

Surround yourself with a support system. Family and friends can make a great circle of support but also think about getting involved in the tech community where you can connect with others learning as you are.
