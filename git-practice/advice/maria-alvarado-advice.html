Hi, my name is Maria and I have some advice for you!

It's ok to make mistakes!

Making mistakes is part of the learning process. The more mistakes you make, the more you learn.

Be consistent!

Even if its only 15 - 30 min, try to practice every day! As any other skill, if you don't practice it, you won't improve.

You don't have to understand everything the first time

Some concepts are difficult to grasp. It doesn't mean you are not smart enough to do it. Everything will make sense the more you practice
