Hi, my name is Hope and I have some advice for you!

Four things to remember with each new project:

  • Don't get discouraged - you are learning a new skill and that takes time and practice. In a world with increasing gratification I have to remind myslef that learning takes time.
  • Take notes - while watching Skillcrush videos and reading the assingments, take notes. This makes you process the info a second time and you will be more likely to remember it.
  • Troubleshoot - Google can answer almost any question so when something gets complicated or confusing, research what info the internet has to offer to help.
  • Ask for help - That is why we have Skillcrush Instructors available. Skillcrush Instructors are quick to respond and extremely knowledgable. When in doubt, let them help!
