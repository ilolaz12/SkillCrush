Hi, my name is Seth Knox and I have some advice for you!

Google is your friend!

No one has every term memorized. While some will become second nature remember that google is your best friend. It's ok and best practice to use resources if you can't remember a step while becoming a front end developer.

Avoid Distractions

I find it easier to focus when I have some quiet, non lyrical music playing while I have noise cancelling headphones on. If I'm feeling fancy I'll have a chai latte ready to sip. Figure out what helps you focus while coding and don't forget to take a break every once in awhile!
