Hi, my name is Berit and I have some advice for you!

Don't be afraid to use other resources!

Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) are great, but sometimes you're left scratching your head. What seems obvious to one person, isn't necessarily obvious to another. Instead of rewatching the same tutorial over and over again, try a competing MOOC. Often, a different person's explanation is all you need to be able to move on. Otherwise:

Good luck and keep coding!
