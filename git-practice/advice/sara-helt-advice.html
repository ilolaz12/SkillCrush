Hi, my name is Sara Helt and I have some advice for you!

Do not give up. Ever. Perserverence is key.

Learning this stuff is hard. Programming and developing is hard. You are learning a new language, and like me, you are probably an adult. Something I have learned though is that "old" dogs can definitely learn new tricks, it just takes extra hard work and focus.

When you are feeling uncomfortable, confused or frustrated, it is easy to say things to yourself like "This career change will never happen for you" or "You are not a developer". The thing is, it really will never happen if you give up.

The biggest risk is taking no risk at all, so keep going!!
