Hi, my name is Cat A. Marsh and I have some advice for you!

Have we Got Clearance, Clarice?

When beginning to learn how to code embrace the idea that you know nothing! It will free you from the pressure of having to be 'perfect' (or even minimally qualified).

Admitting that one doesn't know everything about *everything* can be difficult but curiosity and tenacity will ultimately make you a better coder.

Oveur. Over!

Coding = typ0s. Each fix is an opportunity to deepen your understanding of the development process.

What's Our Vector, Victoria?

Don't be afraid to ask for help. Sometimes the key to fixing an issue is as simple as having a colleague or friend that can spot the error that you missed.
