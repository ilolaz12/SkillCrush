Hi, my name is Willie and here are a few things that I have learned along my journey

Believe in yourself

Throughout my journey I have fallen victim to imposter syndrome a countless number of times. don't listen you deserve to be here! It may be difficult at times, but you are a highly capable developer in the making.

Search for the answers you don't have

Google and YouTube seemed to have been my best friend during this journey. I found that when I didn't fully understand lessons, I could watch a few videos and read artices from differents sources that might explain lessons in a way that is more digestable to me. Usually when I return back to the lesson, I have a better grasp of the material.

Take breaks

If you are anything like me, you want to go, go, go until you learn everything! Make sure that you take some time to do the other things you love so you don't burn out too fast. Especially when you reach a block in your process, taking a step back can allow you to reapproch the problem with a fresh perspective.
